Featured Plants

Monstera Deliciosa

The Monstera Deliciosa is a tropical yet relatively easy plant for beginners to take care of as it is quite sturdy and does not require much for it to survive, even in not so tropical places. However, if you want it to thrive there are some things to think about that will make your plant grow big.

Variegated Monstera Deliciosas

The Monstera Deliciosa Albo Varigate is a type of monstera that is not usually recomended for beginners as it can be expensive to buy and difficult to keep beautiful. It differs from the monstera above in the way that it has white or yellow sections, which is caused by a cell mutation that removes the chlorophyll of the plant. These sections can result in a variety of patterns such as splotches, stripes, dots, blocks and even entire leaves. The lack of chlorophyll in the leaves are what make them difficult as they need extra care in order to not turn brown and die. It is however inevitable that these sections die eventually due to this mutation, but with the right care one can keep them stunning much longer than without it.

Coleus

The Coleus plant has become very popular the last couple of years do to their wide variety in colors, leaf shapes and sizes. It can definitely be considered a beginner plant as it is generally easy to take care of. There are however som beginner mistakes that can really hinder them from showing off their true beauty.
