Monstera Deliciosa

I will tell how to properly care for a monstera deliciosa in not too many words. Plants will bring life to any room in your home, but many will be particularly happy in the bathroom. Ferns like Bertie will love soaking up the steam. Choose a large bushy Bertie for instant impact, or add pops of greenery with a couple of mini ferns.
